🪰 Drosophila Population Generator and χ² Test Simulator

Generate Drosophila populations, perform genetic crosses, and use chi-square tests to evaluate hypotheses of independent assortment.

Teaching note: The three genes are X-linked. Recombination is simulated during female meiosis; males transmit either their X chromosome or Y chromosome.
