DNA Genotyping of Drosophila Mutants: The white-1 Locus (w)

Use this module to read the lab instructions, follow the workflow guide, launch the Virtual PCR tool, and access the assignment for submission.

📘 Lab ManualRead white-locus genotyping, DNA extraction, PCR setup, and gel electrophoresis instructions.🧬 Virtual PCR ToolUpload DNA sequences, run primer combinations, predict PCR products, and build the gel.📝 Fillable AssignmentComplete the assignment in the browser, save progress, and export the finished PDF.

How students should use this module

1

Read the molecular genetics background

Review the white-1 locus, Doc-retroposon insertion, sex-linked inheritance, PCR, and gel electrophoresis.

2

Perform DNA extraction

Work in groups, extract DNA from one fly type, measure sample concentration and purity, and record the values.

3

Set up the PCR reaction

Review the PCR cycler program, select the most suitable conditions, and prepare the reaction as instructed by the TAs.

4

Use the Virtual PCR Tool

Upload the five DNA sequence files, enter PF1, PF2, and PR3, run the required PCRs, and record fragment sizes.

5

Interpret PCR and gel results

Use 467 bp and 704 bp fragments to infer genotypes, phenotypes, heterozygotes, and expected gel bands.

6

Submit the assignment

Complete Assignment #8 and submit a physical copy or scanned PDF through Canvas.
